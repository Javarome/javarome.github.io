I write software for more than three decades now, but I started earlier.

I've worked as a developer, consultant, trainer (including writing courses) and tech lead for teams of developers. My technical experiences would classify me as a full-stack developer, but one that have fun building the front end.

On both sides, I learned a lot, and still am keen to learn more.

Most of my time is not spent searching how to do things, but what is the best (flexible, evoluable, maintainable, testable, scalable) way to do them.

I like creativity, thinking out of the box, always challenge commonly accepted ideas to make sure they still make sense.
